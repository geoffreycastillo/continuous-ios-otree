Payment: a flat fee of $1.25 upon completion of the HIT.

Important Information about the Research Study:

  • The purpose of the study is to investigate Social Preferences. If you choose to participate, you will be asked to answer survey questions on your computer or mobile device.
  • Risks or discomforts from this research include answering questions about potentially sensitive topics and the possibility that we share your anonymized responses to these questions with other mTurk workers in future studies.
  • Taking part in this research project is voluntary. You do not have to participate and you can stop at any time.

Investigators:

  • Benjamin Beranek, Connecticut College (bberanek@conncoll.edu)
  • Geoffrey Castillo, University of Vienna (geoffrey.castillo@univie.ac.at)

Voluntary participation and confidentiality: Participation in this study is entirely voluntary, and may be withdrawn at any given moment without further consequences. All decisions will be made anonymously.

Risks: There are some risks you might experience from being in this study. One risk is being asked about potentially sensitive subjects. Another risk is the possibility of a breach of confidentiality. We might use your anonymized responses to the survey questions in future studies and it is possible that someone with high levels of technical skill and effort could determine your identity. We think this possibility is quite low and we will make efforts, should we use your responses, to keep them as anonymous as possible.

Detailed Informed Consent Information: You can download more detailed Informed Consent documentation here.

Informed consent: By accepting this HIT you give us informed consent that we can use your answers in anonymized form for research purposes only.
